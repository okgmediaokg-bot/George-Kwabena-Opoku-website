About George

Biography

George Kwabena Opoku is a Ghanaian technology professional, entrepreneur, youth leader, and creative thinker dedicated to leveraging digital innovation for transformation and empowerment.

With an academic background in Information Technology and certifications in cybersecurity, data analytics, and AI productivity, George has developed strong competencies in digital systems, leadership, communication, and strategic thinking.

Beyond technology, he is deeply passionate about youth mentorship, public speaking, writing, fitness, and faith-based community service. He is also the author of an upcoming autobiography titled “From Kanda to the Courtroom.”
